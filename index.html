01 · About

Researcher.
Developer. Educator.

I am a MS Data Science student at Sabanci University, Istanbul, where I work under Dr. Erchan Aptoula in the Computer Vision and Pattern Analysis Laboratory. My thesis focuses on open-set classification via uncertainty quantification.

My research centers on making deep learning models robust to novel, unseen classes — bridging evidential deep learning, hyperbolic geometry, and spectral-spatial feature disentanglement for hyperspectral image analysis.

I completed my BSc in Computer Engineering at Bilkent University (Ankara), including an Erasmus semester at Roskilde University in Denmark. Alongside research, I serve as a Teaching Assistant across undergraduate and graduate-level courses at Sabanci.
